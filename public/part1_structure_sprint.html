This is my favorite recipe as it is so easy to make, comforting, and packed with flavor !

Ingredients ğ”ŒÕ. .Õğ¦¯

  • Pack of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chili oil
  • 1-2 sheets of nori

â‚^. .^â‚âŸ† Instructions

  1. Bring the broth to a simmer in a medium pot
  2. Add the soy sauce, chili oil, and sesame oil. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add noodles and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender(I like overcooked noodles)
  4. While waiting, start cutting your green onions (if you havent sliced them yet)
  5. Grab a separate bowl and boil an egg
  6. Pour the noodles and broth into a bowl.
  7. Top with the egg, sliced green onions, and torn or cut pieces of nori
  8. Enjoy!
